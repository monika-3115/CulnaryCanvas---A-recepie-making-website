Easy Cheesecake Pie


This easy cheesecake pie is perfect for cheesecake lovers who want a quicker, easier alternative to making a classic cheesecake. The smooth and creamy filling is made of just 6 simple ingredients, and there’s no springform pan or water bath step required. It’s delicious on its own, or you can top it with raspberry sauce, whipped cream, lemon curd, fresh berries, or salted caramel.




Easy Cheesecake Pie


Texture:

The texture of an Easy Cheesecake Pie is smooth, creamy, and velvety. It has a firm yet light consistency that melts in your mouth with each bite. The crust adds a bit of crunchiness, complementing the creamy filling perfectly.


Flavor:

Easy Cheesecake Pie offers a rich and indulgent flavor profile. The cream cheese provides a tangy, slightly sour taste that is balanced by the sweetness of the sugar. Depending on the recipe and personal preference, you can customize the flavor with additions like vanilla extract, lemon zest, or even chocolate swirls. The crust, often made from graham crackers or digestive biscuits, adds a buttery and slightly sweet contrast to the creamy filling.


Ease:

As the name suggests, Easy Cheesecake Pie is designed to be simple to make. It usually involves mixing together a few basic ingredients for the crust, pressing it into a pie dish, then whipping up the cheesecake filling which often consists of cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and flavorings like vanilla extract or lemon juice. Once the filling is poured over the crust, it's baked until set. Some recipes may even skip the baking step altogether and rely on gelatin or other setting agents to firm up the filling.


Time:

One of the appeals of an Easy Cheesecake Pie is its simplicity and quick preparation time compared to traditional cheesecakes. Depending on the recipe, it typically takes around 15-20 minutes to prepare the filling and crust, and then around 25-30 minutes to bake. Chilling time after baking varies but usually takes a few hours to overnight.



Ingredients


Easy Cheesecake Pie


For the crust:


1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
6 tablespoons of melted butter
3 tablespoons of granulated sugar (optional, for added sweetness)

For Filling:


16 ounces (2 packages) of cream cheese, softened
½ cup of granulated sugar
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
Optional: lemon zest or juice for a hint of citrus flavor









1.Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar (if using). Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with butter.

2.Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish, forming an even crust. You can use the back of a spoon or a flat-bottomed glass to help press the crumbs firmly into place. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until it is lightly golden and set. Remove from the oven and let it cool while you prepare the filling.

3.In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy. Add the granulated sugar and beat until well combined and smooth.

4.Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until fully incorporated. Mix in the vanilla extract and any additional flavorings, such as lemon zest or juice, if desired. Make sure everything is well combined and smooth. Pour the cheesecake filling into the cooled crust, spreading it out evenly.

5.Place the pie dish on a baking sheet (to catch any potential drips) and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set around the edges but still slightly jiggly in the center. Remove the cheesecake pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature on a wire rack. Then, refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to chill and set completely.

6.Once chilled and set, slice the Easy Cheesecake Pie into wedges and serve chilled. You can optionally garnish each slice with whipped cream, fresh berries, or a drizzle of fruit sauce for an extra special touch. Enjoy your delicious and easy-to-make Cheesecake Pie!
