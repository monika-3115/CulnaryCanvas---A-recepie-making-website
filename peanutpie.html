Peanut Butter Ice Cream Pie


This 6-ingredient peanut butter ice cream pie combines an Oreo cookie crust, extra creamy peanut butter ice cream filling, swirls of peanut butter, and lots of Reese’s Pieces. It’s the perfect go-to summer dessert recipe because it’s astoundingly easy to make, includes ice cream, and most of the prep is hands-off, as the freezer does the work! 100% guaranteed to be gobbled up, and everyone will request you make it again and again.




Peanut Butter Ice Cream Pie Details:


Texture:

The texture of Peanut Butter Ice Cream Pie is wonderfully creamy and smooth, thanks to the velvety consistency of the peanut butter-infused ice cream. The crust adds a satisfying crunch that contrasts nicely with the creamy filling, creating a delightful textural contrast with every bite. As the pie freezes, the ice cream firms up, resulting in a sliceable dessert that holds its shape well when sliced and served.


Flavor:

The flavor of Peanut Butter Ice Cream Pie is dominated by the rich, nutty taste of peanut butter. It typically features layers of creamy peanut butter ice cream infused with peanut butter swirls or chunks, all nestled in a crunchy cookie or graham cracker crust. Some recipes also incorporate toppings like chocolate ganache or chopped peanuts to enhance the flavor profile..


Ease:

Peanut Butter Ice Cream Pie is relatively easy to make, especially if you opt for a no-bake version that doesn't require baking the crust. The most time-consuming part is typically preparing the crust and allowing it to chill or bake, depending on the recipe. The filling is usually a simple mixture of peanut butter and softened ice cream, which is then poured into the prepared crust and frozen until set. Overall, it's a straightforward dessert that doesn't require advanced baking skills.


Time:

The total time required to make Peanut Butter Ice Cream Pie can vary depending on the specific recipe you choose. A basic version might take around 20-30 minutes of active preparation time, plus several hours of freezing time to allow the pie to set completely.



Ingredients


Peanut Butter Ice Cream Pie:


For the crust:


1 ½ cups crushed graham crackers or cookie crumbs
6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
2 tablespoons granulated sugar


Filling Ingredients:


1 ½ cups creamy peanut butter
1 quart (4 cups) vanilla or chocolate ice cream, softened



Optional Toppings:


Chocolate sauce, melted
Chopped peanuts
Whipped cream





1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) if you're using a baked crust. In a mixing bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers or cookie crumbs with melted butter and sugar (if using). Stir until the mixture resembles wet sand and holds together when pressed.

2.Press the mixture firmly into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish to form the crust. If baking, bake the crust for 10-12 minutes until lightly golden. Allow it to cool completely before proceeding. In a microwave-safe bowl or on the stovetop, gently heat the peanut butter until it's soft and easy to stir. Be careful not to overheat it.

3.In a large mixing bowl, combine the softened peanut butter with the softened ice cream. Stir until well combined and smooth. Pour the peanut butter and ice cream mixture into the cooled pie crust, smoothing the top with a spatula.

4.Place the pie in the freezer and let it freeze until firm, which usually takes at least 4-6 hours or overnight. Before serving, you can drizzle melted chocolate sauce over the top of the pie and sprinkle with chopped peanuts if desired.

5.If using, garnish each slice with a dollop of whipped cream. Use a sharp knife dipped in hot water to slice the pie easily. Enjoy!
