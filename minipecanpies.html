Mini Pecan Pies


These mini pecan pies are one of my favourite recipies. Made with my favorite homemade pie crust and a salted pecan filling, they’re always a welcome salty-sweet addition to any holiday dessert spread.Pecan pie is a quintessential Thanksgiving pie. Though nothing will ever beat classic apple pie or creamy pumpkin pie in my mind, traditional pecan pie holds a very special place in my heart.




Mini Pecan Pies


Texture:

The texture of mini pecan pies is typically a combination of crunchy and gooey. The pecans on top provide a satisfying crunch, while the filling underneath, usually made with ingredients like brown sugar, corn syrup, and eggs, creates a soft and slightly gooey center. The crust adds another layer of texture, usually flaky and buttery.


Flavor:

Mini pecan pies boast a rich, nutty flavor with caramel undertones. The combination of pecans, brown sugar, and butter creates a sweet, indulgent taste that's balanced by the slight bitterness of the pecans. Additionally, the use of ingredients like vanilla extract and a pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor profile, adding depth to the sweetness.


Ease:

Mini pecan pies are relatively easy to prepare, especially if you use pre-made pie crusts. The filling typically involves mixing together ingredients like chopped pecans, brown sugar, corn syrup, eggs, butter, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Once the filling is prepared, it's spooned into mini pie crusts and topped with additional pecan halves for decoration. The most time-consuming part of the process is often preparing the pie crusts if you're making them from scratch. However, using store-bought crusts can significantly reduce prep time. Overall, mini pecan pies require minimal equipment and can be baked in a muffin tin, making them a convenient dessert option for various occasions.


Time:

The time required to make mini pecan pies can vary depending on whether you're making the pie crust from scratch or using store-bought crusts. Overall, you can expect to spend around 1.5 to 2 hours from start to finish, including prep, baking, and cooling time.



Ingredients


Mini Pecan Pies


For the crust:


All-purpose flour: 1 1/4 cups (156g)
Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
Cold unsalted butter: 1/2 cup (113g), cut into small cubes
Ice water: 3 to 4 tablespoons

For Filling:


Pecans, chopped: 1 cup (120g)
Pecan halves for decoration (optional)
Brown sugar: 1/2 cup (100g), packed
Corn syrup (light or dark): 1/2 cup (120ml)
Eggs: 2 large
Unsalted butter, melted: 2 tablespoons (28g)
Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
Salt: 1/4 teaspoon









1.In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Add the cold cubed butter to the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, blend the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

2.Gradually add ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Place a rack in the middle position.

3.In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped pecans, brown sugar, corn syrup, eggs, melted butter, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until well combined. Roll out the chilled pie dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/8-inch thickness. Use a round cookie cutter or glass slightly larger than the mini pie tins to cut out circles of dough.

4.Press each dough circle into the mini pie tins, making sure to cover the bottom and sides. Spoon the pecan filling into each mini pie crust, filling them about three-quarters full.

5.If desired, decorate the tops of the pies with pecan halves. Place the mini pecan pies on a baking sheet and transfer them to the preheated oven.

6.Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown. Allow the mini pecan pies to cool in the tins for about 10-15 minutes.

7.Carefully remove them from the tins and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Serve the mini pecan pies warm or at room temperature, optionally topped with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.
